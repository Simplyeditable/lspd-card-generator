How to use?

  1. Fill out all the required fields in the generator above.
  2. Click the "Generate & Download Card" button to save the image to your computer.
  3. Go to a service like Imgur and upload the downloaded image to get a link.
  4. Go to any 24/7 in-game and buy notes.
  5. Use the command: /note [id of blank note] [amount] [name] LSPD Card F. Lastname
  6. Use the command: /note [id of new note] [amount] [content] [Your Imgur URL]
  7. Use the command: /giveitem to give out your cards to other people.
